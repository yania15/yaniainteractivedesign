Origins

Helvetica was originally developed by Max Miedinger and released by Linotype in 1957. However, its sister company called D. Stempel AG released the Neue Helvetica font design, a rendition of the original.

Helvetica is a sans-serif font that has been widely used for many applications such as advertising.
